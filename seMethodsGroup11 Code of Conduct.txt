seMethodsGroup11 Code of Conduct

1. All team members should be respected.
2. All team members should contribute to the project. 
3. If a team member wants to change something then they should consult the rest of the team first.
4. If a team member cannot make it to a meeting or cannot do the work before the deadline they should give a long enough notice so the team can help or fix the problem.
5. The team should have weekly meetings.
6. The team should listen to each others ideas.
